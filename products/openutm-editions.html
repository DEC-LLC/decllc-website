On HA and state sync. Community, Standard, and Professional include a limited HA + state-sync implementation suitable for small deployments and lab work. Enterprise and above add the advanced state-sync path — low-latency cross-node replication, session resume across failover, and the throughput headroom to hold sync at 25+ Gbps without lag.

1 DEC SDNS cross-product link-up is a paid add-on SKU that enables an AI Quorum between two DEC-LLC core products — multi-product AI agents reaching consensus on coordinated actions across your infrastructure. Priced at $3K / yr per link-up between two products, or $15K / yr for the full bundle (all 6 links among OpenUTM / IVMIA / NIVMIA / VaultSync). Requires Professional tier or higher on both products in the link-up. Not available at Community or Standard. Why a fee: SDNS pricing underwrites the cyber / IT / business-E&O insurance coverage DEC-LLC carries for cross-product orchestration — coordinated action carries liability, and the fee funds the insurance that covers it.
